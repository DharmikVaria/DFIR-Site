Reference Layer

Trusted DFIR and Cybercrime Resources

Strong investigators rely on credible references, official reporting channels, standards, tool documentation, and practitioner research. This page collects resources useful for Indian cybercrime awareness, DFIR learning, and professional methodology.

How to evaluate forensic resources

Prefer official documentation, standards bodies, tool authors, recognized practitioners, and repeatable technical research. Be careful with outdated blogs because artifacts change across operating system versions, application updates, and cloud service changes.

For legal or victim reporting matters, use official government channels rather than social media advice. For technical learning, validate claims by testing artifacts in a lab whenever possible.
